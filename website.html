My Robot: MIRTO

MIRTO is a robot that was built by Middelsex students and staff. It is used for teaching programming.

What can MIRTO do?

  • Move forwards
  • Move backwards
  • Turn around
  • Play a note
  • Send messages to LCD screen

What I have programmed MIRTO to do?

  • I coded MIRTO to compete in a race.
  • I have coded MIRTO move in the various shapes, e.g. square, cirlce.
  • I have coded MIRTO to move in the shape of a house.
  • I have coded MIRTO to move in a unique dance/music sequence
  • I have coded MIRTO to move in various ways by pressing keys

What I wish I could program MIRTO to do?

  • I could code MIRTO to move around the classroom independently, avoiding obstacles.
  • I could code MIRTO to follow a line.
  • I could code MIRTO to communicate with humans.
  • I could code MIRTO to copy what I do.
